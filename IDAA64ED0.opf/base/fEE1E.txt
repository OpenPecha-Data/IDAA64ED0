bhagavān āha | tat kiṃ manyase subhūte api nu tathāgatasyaivaṃ bhavati na 1 mayā dharmo deśita iti | yaḥ subhūte evaṃ vad
et tathāgatena dharmo deśita iti | abhyācakṣīta māṃ sa subhūte asatād udgṛhītena |tat kasya hetor dharmadeśanā dharmadeś
aneti subhūte nāsti sa kaścit dharmo yo dharmadeśanā nāmopalabhyate |
